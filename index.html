Our story

The idea that kept calling me back

Three years ago, I had an idea. It felt bigger than me, a candle that could carry meaning, that could fund something real for women who needed it. I didn't know how to bring it to life, and more than once, I almost let it go.

But it kept calling me back. Late at night, in quiet moments, the vision wouldn't leave. So I started small, pouring candles by hand, testing the idea at a local event to see if it meant anything to anyone besides me.

The response was overwhelming. People felt it too. That's when I knew Femi9 Energy wasn't just a candle. It was a movement waiting to happen.

Today, Femi9 Energy is proud to be sponsored by Nation of Diversity and featured in their Mental Health Awareness Self-Care Toolkit. This is just the beginning. Mariama
