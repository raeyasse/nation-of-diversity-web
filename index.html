Why we exist

More than a candle

Behind every flame is a commitment to uplift women who lack access to basic care.

🩹

Feminine Care

Proceeds fund menstrual products, pads, tampons, and feminine hygiene essentials for women in shelters and underserved communities.

💊

Health Resources

We partner with local clinics and nonprofits to ensure women get access to reproductive health education and basic care supplies.

🤝

Community First

Every batch is poured locally. We keep the supply chain close so we can invest more directly into the women we serve.

The model

You buy. She receives.

50% of every candle purchase goes directly into our women's care fund. No middlemen. No bureaucracy. Just impact.

🕯️

Our story

Born from passion & purpose

femi9_energy started in a kitchen with a pot, some wax, and a big vision — candles that do more than smell good.

Every candle is hand-poured in small batches with love, intention, and clean ingredients. No corners cut. No shortcuts taken.

As a woman-owned small business, we know firsthand how powerful community care can be — and we're committed to channeling that energy into every pour.
